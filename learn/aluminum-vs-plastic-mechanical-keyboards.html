Aluminum vs Plastic Mechanical Keyboards: Sound, Feel, and Price

A die-cast aluminum case adds weight and rigidity that typically produce a deeper, more consolidated sound and a more solid typing feel than a plastic case — at a higher price. Plastic keeps boards light and affordable and can still sound great when well engineered.
